Spicy Chicken Pasta

Description:

This dish is a delicious blend of a garlic based, smokey tomato sauce paired with cheesy pasta and grilled chicken breast

Ingredients:

  • Chili Powder
  • Oregano
  • Garlic
  • Coriander
  • Hot Sauce
  • Butter
  • Minced Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Low Fat Cream Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Mozzarella Cheese

How to Prepare This Dish

Sauce:

  1. Add 30g of butter to warm saucepan, let it melt
  2. Add 150g of tomato sauce, mix
  3. Add 1 tbsp of minced garlic, chili powder, oregano, garlic, coriander and hot sauce of your choice
  4. Add 40g of low fat cream cheese
  5. Let your sauce simmer, stirring occasionally while you prepare your chicken

Chicken:

  1. Coat your chicken breast with your hot sauce
  2. Season with 1 tbsp of chili powder, oregano, garlic, coriander
  3. Cook your chicken (recommended grilling or baking) until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Once your chicken is cooked, shred or slice and add it to your sauce

Pasta:

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Add your pasta, boil until tender
  3. Add your cooked pasta to your sauce, mixing it well with the sauce and chicken
  4. Then add shredded mozzarella cheese, mixing it well until melted
  5. Enjoy!
